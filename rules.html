Rules

Draft Day: Each team picks 2 full-time drivers in a randomized snake pattern.

However many points your drivers score, you earn as points for the weekend.

Teams may trade any driver or swap out with an unpicked driver at any point during the season, except during an IndyCar event.

Before the beginning of the season, all teams predict the champion.

Before the green flag each weekend, all teams predict the winner.

100 bonus points are awarded to whoever correctly predicts the champion.

25 bonus points are awarded to whoever correctly guesses the winner on a given weekend.
